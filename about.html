About Us

PT Sigma Inovasi Global (SIG) Founded in 2023, we are a privately owned company based in East Java, Indonesia, committed to delivering high-quality cosmetic ingredients to formulators and brands across the region. With a strong focus on innovation, passion, and craftsmanship, we strive to empower the beauty and personal care industry through reliable sourcing, technical expertise, and forwardthinking solutions that meet evolving consumer demands. Our goal is to become a trusted partner in your product development journey, offering not only ingredients but also insights and inspiration to help bring your formulations to life.

Our Facilities

ADVANCED LABORATORY

To support our customers' formulation and development needs, we also operate an advanced in-house laboratory equipped for product testing, stability trials, and prototype development. This facility allows us to provide hands-on technical support, ensuring our clients can confidently bring high-performance cosmetic products to market.

WAREHOUSE

Our warehouse, strategically located in the SIRIE Industrial Estate in East Java, is designed for efficient storage and distribution, ensuring fast, safe, and consistent delivery of ingredients to our partners.
